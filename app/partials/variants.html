Variant definitions

We predict the consequence of variants (germline or somatic) on genes associated with diseases based on the evidence for the Genetic associations and Somatic mutation data types. This effect is defined by consequence terms from the Sequence Ontology (SO) project.

The SO consequence terms, their descriptions and accession IDs are shown in the table below. The terms are shown in decreasing order of severity (from more severe to less severe) based on the functional score in Koscielny et al (Supplementary Table 2).
